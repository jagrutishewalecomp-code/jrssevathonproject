# V-ORBIT Brand Guidelines

## Logo Design Specifications
- **Logos in Use:** Include primary and secondary logo options.
- **Clear Space:** Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "V".
- **Logo Variations:** Use color versions on light backgrounds and monochrome versions on dark backgrounds.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Colors:**
  - **Vibrant Blue:** #007BFF
  - **Bright Orange:** #FF5733
- **Secondary Colors:**
  - **Light Gray:** #F0F0F0
  - **Dark Gray:** #333333

## Typography
- **Headings:**
  - **Font:** Montserrat, Bold
  - **Size:** 24px
- **Body Text:**
  - **Font:** Open Sans, Regular
  - **Size:** 16px

## Visual Identity Standards
- **Imagery:** Use images that reflect innovation, creativity, and technology.
- **Tone of Voice:** Maintain a professional yet approachable tone.

- Include examples of proper and improper logo usage, color applications, and typography in practice.
